How Caddy Buddy Works

1Golfer logs in and requests a tee time along with a caddy for a specific date.
2Caddy has option to confirm the reservation. Once confirmed, that specific tee time will no longer be available.
3Reminders will be sent to both the caddies and golfers about their confirmed tee times through SMS.
